Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a confirmed medical diagnosis?

Absolutely not. This tool uses Machine Learning patterns simply to calculate if a subset of symptoms overlaps with known pediatric cancers. Only a hematologic laboratory sequence and a certified oncologist can officially diagnose cancer.

Why isn't model gender-specific?

To prevent algorithmic discrimination. Many healthcare models historically harbor deep biases regarding gender or demographics. We ensure the **Universal Baseline** considers only raw clinical indicators.

How is the ML threshold set?

The "Low/Medium/High" boundaries rely on an ensemble hybrid model (`GradForest`). When highly predictive "red flag" symptoms (like severe bone pain waking a child at night) align, the urgency calculation spikes appropriately.

Is my data saved?

No personal identifying data is logged. All checklist computations currently run securely inside your local browser context via JavaScript.
