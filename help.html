Recommended Steps

1 Do Not Panic

Many cancer-like symptoms overlap heavily with common childhood viral infections, growth pains, or minor injuries. Stay calm.

2 Record Observations

Log the exact duration of the fever, pain, or lumps. Pediatricians rely heavily on timelines to rule out standard viruses.

3 Primary Checkup

Visit your regular pediatrician first. They will initiate standard Complete Blood Count (CBC) tests if they deem it necessary.

4 Specialist Referral

Only if tests show irregularities will you be formally referred to a Pediatric Oncologist at a specialized facility below.
